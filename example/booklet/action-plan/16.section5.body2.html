Utilize Accounts For Customer Management & Payments

The web application could have "services" for sale, in a store-like presentation where potential clients can purchase services.

Depending on how you do business, this can be either an initial payment. With the ability to issue the client further invoices through their account.

This system could also function as your point-of-sale (POS) system, by adding a web application and a tablet with a card reader. Or enable you to allow customers to pay over time, in a payment plan. There is a of flexibility, and we can make it suited to your needs as the forums grow.

A full client management system, tracking all clients:

  • Potential clients, tracking messages between you and them, all in one place, with their contact details, and accessible when you make contact over the phone for example.
  • Active clients, tracking what has been done, what needs to be done, and ability to mark their case as complete and issue their bill or even notify them how much they won; this could be visible on the forums which would greaterly influence others to use your services (optional, and with the user's permission).
  • Past clients, who may become clients again, supporting a referal system, where they can get compensated, or at the very least you know how you came to know this client, which could help with small talk or getting in touch with the client.

    • Start from tracking potential client leads, tracking all active clients and have a system to track and monitor the stage of their cases, and store information about previous clients after scrubbing all sensitive data.

And we do everything we can so that in the event of an attack, the amount of data lost is negligble as possible using cutting edge techniques, and approaching software development as a science and not a trade skill.
